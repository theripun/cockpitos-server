# 🎬 COCKPIT: THE CLOUD COMPUTER
### *Investor & Partner Presentation Script*
### *Duration: ~16-18 Minutes*
### *Goal: Explain the value, simplicity, and market potential without technical jargon.*

---

## 📋 PRODUCTION NOTES
- **Visual Style**: Clean, high-end, "Apple-like" product shots. Avoid showing raw code unless it's to contrast "the old way" with Cockpit.
- **Narrator Tone**: Confident, visionary, yet grounded. Think "engaging tech documentary" rather than "sales pitch."
- **Key Metaphor**: "The Cloud Computer" — treating a server not as a piece of infrastructure, but as a personal computer that lives on the internet.
- **Screen Recording Tips**: Show real UI, real interactions. Hover states, bouncy dock animations, and glass effects are the hero visuals.
